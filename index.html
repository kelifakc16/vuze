Artist Journey

What's wrong with music industry now ?

Record labels come in all shapes and sizes, and so do their contracts with artists. More often than not, these contracts are unfair, unbreakable, and sometimes even debt inducing. All as a means to secure artists for as long as possible.

Young and talented artists are faced with an ultimatum. Sign or die. Signing means forfeiting large shares of royalties, distribution rights, ownership, and even creative direction of their music. Going independent comes with the problem of finding funding for equipment, marketing, instruments, studio space, and software to create music in the first place.

We seek to change that by offering an alternative for upcoming and aspiring artists.

How do we solve this?

Artists can gain the necessary funding they need by offering a percentage of their album on our site. We then take that percentage and mint ‘tokens’, each representing a small percentage of the album.

Fans and investors alike can then buy these tokens, giving them the rights to some of them royalties and the opportunity to trade amongst themselves much like a regular exchange. When these tokens are bought up, the artist directly gets funding.

Furthermore, everytime a token is traded, whether it be from person to person or bought for the first time, a small percentage of the price it was traded for goes back to the artist, offering a lifetime revenue stream for the work.
